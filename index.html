Setup

Update names and colors below, then press Start Game. The chosen grade controls the starting wallet and the savings goal.

Setup Checklist

  1. Choose grade level.
  2. Choose number of players.
  3. Add player names.
  4. Pick token colors.
  5. Press Start Game.

Smartboard Tip

After setup, switch to Game Board for play, Players for progress checks, and Teacher when you need overrides.

Player Profiles

Each scout keeps a wallet for spending and savings for winning. The current player is highlighted.

How to Play

  1. Start every player on Start / Allowance.
  2. Roll the die and move around the board.
  3. Follow the space you land on.
  4. Wallet money can be spent.
  5. Savings money helps you win.
  6. Only money in savings counts toward the goal.
  7. Buy spaces let you draw 4 Buy Cards and choose one or skip all.
  8. Bank spaces let you move wallet money into savings.
  9. Giving is optional and positive.
  10. The first player to reach the savings goal wins.
  11. No loans, no debt, no bankruptcy, no rent, no jail, and no elimination.
  12. Scouts win by saving wisely.
